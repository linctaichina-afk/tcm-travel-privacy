Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We only access data that is strictly necessary for the App's core functionality. This includes:

  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth tokens required to post content to your connected social media accounts.
  • Content Metadata: Video descriptions, titles, and scheduling information that you provide through the App.

2. How We Use Information

All data is used solely for the purpose of executing your requested content publishing tasks. We do not analyze, sell, or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally and encrypted. We do not store your content on our servers beyond what is necessary to complete the publishing process.

4. Third-Party Services

The App connects to third-party platforms (TikTok, YouTube) through their official APIs. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle your data.

5. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon disconnecting your accounts, all associated tokens and data will be removed.
